Kim Hanke, Founder and CEO

Kim is a serial entrepreneur with over four decades of leadership experience across design, engineering, business, process, training, production and manufacturing.

Former Director & National Chairperson, Women’s Automotive Association.

Expert in product validation, process improvement strategic planning, training, Computer Aided Design and Product Life-cycle / Data Management Systems. Specializing in Automotive, Aerospace, Defense, and Commercial Manufacturing.
